Poster and Packging Design


Art Inspired Posters

For our History of Communication class, we were tasked with creating three different packaging and posters that represented or emulated the style of art movements we have seen in the past such as Rococo, Impressionism, and Neo classism. The posters created are promotional posters that advertise different products. Packaging was the main task as it had to showcase the designated movement. I chose to create a pill advertisement with the style of Rococo as the context and time significance was quite fitting. I proceed to make a matchstick with neoclassicism motifs as the movement was quite power oriented. Lastly, I chose impressionism, where I was inspired by VanGoghâ€™s style to create packaging for a coffee cup. The posters were designed on Illustrator using different tools such as opacity, clipmask, pencil and pen, gradient and much more. Mockups of each packaging were created on Photoshop
