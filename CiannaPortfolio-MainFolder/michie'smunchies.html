Promotional Campaign For Fictional Brand.



Michie's Munchies- A fictional Brand

This project was one of my Final First Year Projects. The project entails a fictional brand that was inspired by my mother and her passion for Baking. Our assignment’s aim was to create a campaign to promote a brand of our choosing, real or fiction. Michie’s Munchies is created as a cookie brand. The campaign created contained a range of posters, bus branding, hoardings, box packaging and much more including a promotional video. This project was created using different software's such as Illustrator for graphics and packaging, Photoshop for image editing and mockups, In Design for brochure design and finally video editing with Premier Pro
